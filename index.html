Skater Information:

- StarSkate skaters must wear appropriate clothing on both on and off-ice sessions.
- Form-fitting skating clothing (sweaters, leggings or practice skirts, practice dresses, gloves) is required to assess body alignment.
- Skaters should conduct a warm-up and a cool-down (can be on or off-ice, with or without a coach).

StarSkate Etiquette:

- Skaters practicing their solo with music have first priority on the session.
- Skaters in lessons have second priority on the session.
- Skaters must be aware of their surroundings; safety first.
- Skaters are to jump along the sides of the rink and spin in the middle during practice, apart from solo program run-throughs.
