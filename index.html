Parent Information:

- Invoices will be billed at the end of the month unless otherwise specified.
- Invoices should be paid within 2 weeks of billing (unless I am informed in advance of any late payments) otherwise lessons will pause until the payment is received.
- Any outstanding bills to any previous coaches MUST be paid prior to continuing any lessons with me; this is part of our Coaches Code of Ethics.
- Length of lessons will vary based on coach-parent communication. Available lessons include private, semi-private, and group lessons (Star 1-3).
- If a skater is unable to attend a lesson, please notify the coach at least 24 hours in advance, otherwise you will still be charged for the lesson.
- In the event of an emergency, please at least inform me prior to the start of the lesson regarding cancellation.

Skater Information:

- StarSkate skaters must wear appropriate clothing on both on and off-ice sessions.
- Form-fitting skating clothing (sweaters, leggings or practice skirts, practice dresses, gloves) is required to assess body alignment.
- Skaters should conduct a warm-up and a cool-down (can be on or off-ice, with or without a coach).
- Please fill out Skater Information form.

StarSkate Etiquette:

- Skaters practicing their solo with music have first priority on the session.
- Skaters in lessons have second priority on the session.
- Skaters must be aware of their surroundings; safety first.
- Skaters are to jump along the sides of the rink and spin in the middle during practice, apart from solo program run-throughs.
